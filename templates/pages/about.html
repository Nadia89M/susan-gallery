About Me

Few Words About me

I create beautiful, handmade and unique headwear, offering a contemporary reinvention of vintage style for any occasion.

My love of hats began as a child when I fell in love with the glamorous, feminine, elegance of the movie stars of the 1930 and 1940s.

I studied at the London College of Fashion. After enjoying a successful career in the fashion and retail industry I decided to embrace to my love of hats by setting up as a milliner in 2013.

In addition to making hats for weddings and the races I have designed for the theatre and created unique pieces for fashion shoots.

Get in Touch »

While I am trained in both classical and vintage millinery techniques, I am also self-taught in vintage hat construction having collected a range of vintage hats which I use for inspiration and research. Every item is beautifully handmade to order. All hats are entirely hand-stitched by me. I never use a sewing machine, preferring to employ couture techniques. I also collect vintage ribbons, fabrics and trimmings for my clients, so that each piece really is one of a kind.

My personal inspirations include classic films, English garden flowers, art and vintage style. But what matters to me most is working closely with clients to create something really special to them. I take time to discuss how you want to look and feel and each hat is designed with love to flatter your face and complement your outfit. Find out more about how the bespoke process works here. I also offer a small number of ready-to-wear hats and can remodel and trim existing hats. Or, if you have a precious vintage hat that has seen better days, I can restore, remodel or even reproduce it.

I live in leafy South East London with my photographer husband, our cats Monty and Betty and our miniature schnauzer Rita, named after Rita Hayworth, of course. “I believe that every woman can look wonderful in a hat. And if you think you can’t, you just haven’t met the right hat yet.”
