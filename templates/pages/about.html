About Me

Few Words About me

After studying at The London College of Fashion in the early 1980s I worked in the fashion and retail industry for over 30 years before deciding to pursue my love of hats by creating bespoke pieces in 2013.
Drawing inspiration from my collection of vintage millinery and a long held love of the classic films of the 1930s and 1940s and the glamorous clothes the stars wore in them, I fashion individual styles using unique vintage trimmings and fabrics so that each piece has a completely individual look.
I work closely with clients to create something really special in colours to match specific fabrics or outfits and taking time to discuss what occasion they are buying for and how they want to look and feel.
I live in leafy South East London with my photographer husband and our cats Monty and Betty and miniature schnauzer Rita.
