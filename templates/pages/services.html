Bespoke Process

THE BESPOKE PROCESS

The word 'bespoke' can be daunting, but all it means is that your hat, headpiece or headband is designed and made especially for you. The process may be less time consuming than trailing round endless shops trying on hats or buying and sending back from websites. That's especially the case if you want to match a particular outfit or don't feel confident in hats. I enjoy helping you find the hat that make you feel wonderful, and I'm always here if you have questions or want to discuss changes. And you may find the price is competitive with some ready-to-wear hats. The feedback from my customers is that having a hat made for them is enjoyable, creative and fun.  My clients tell me there's little as thrilling as owning something entirely unique.  Having something made bespoke for you is a very special feeling. Here's how the process works:

FROM APPOINTMENT TO DELIVERY

First, we have an initial, no-obligation, informal conversation of around 30-40 minutes. During this time you can tell me about your event, preferred colours, colour, the outfit you are wearing, your style and preferences.  You can come to my workshop, I can come to you if you are in London, or we can meet in central London. Please bring fabric swatches or your outfit if you want a hat to match.

I will also ask how much you prefer to spend and discuss the type of fabrics and trimmings available at different price-points. I always endeavour to work within a budget that suits you.

After this meeting, I can give you a quotation, and if you wish to place an order, I will take a 25% deposit.

Around 2-3 weeks later we will have a second meeting to try on your hat, so it fits precisely, and to ensure you are happy with the style.

At the third and final meeting, usually around 6 weeks after initial order, I will show you the completed hat and make any final adjustments. Payment of the balance is required at this point.

All my hats are wrapped in tissue and ribbon and beautifully presented to take home. If you require a hatbox, this can also be arranged.

Otherwise, your hat can be posted in protective packaging. This usually costs between £10 and £20, depending on the size.

Because all my hats are hand-sewn, please allow six weeks for the process. Demand can be higher in summer, so for weddings and Ascot, please plan early. However, if you need something sooner, don't hesitate to call me to discuss as rush orders can sometimes be made.

FOR CUSTOMERS OUT OF LONDON

While meeting in person is always the best option, it is also possible to work successfully on the phone and by email. You can post swatches of material that you'd like me to match, and I can also post samples of fabrics and the final hat to you.

SIZING

My headpieces and hairbands are made in one size to fit everyone. They are shaped and formed with wire and have elastic so may be adjusted to fit any head size perfectly.

However, hats are made-to-measure and traditionally blocked so, I will either need to measure your head, or you can send your measurements to me.

HOW TO MEASURE

Use a soft tape measure. Measure your head straight across your forehead just above your ears and around the fullest part of your head.
